Beyond Diagnosis

We are tired of constantly hearing politicians, policy wonks, and pundits diagnose the issues with our healthcare and economic system without prescribing clear solutions. This blog is a space for going beyond just diagnosing — a place for leftist healthcare analysis and democratic socialist perspectives from the NYC-DSA Healthcare Working Group that offer clear solutions and are wholeheartedly pro-patient and pro-worker.
